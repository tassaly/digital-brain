# Growing IronHub Userbase to More Energy Companies

This project focuses on expanding the reach and adoption of the IronHub platform among energy companies, converting the recent surge in marketplace activity into tangible user growth and sales.

## Project Overview
- **Goal:** Expand the IronHub userbase by onboarding more energy companies and converting high marketing activity (e.g., the 170% growth in marketplace views seen in Q1 2026) into active platform users and sales.
- **Current Status:** Active focus area for Q2 2026.
- **Key Strategies:**
  - **Bridge the Conversion Gap:** Develop and execute sales strategies to turn organic search traffic, LinkedIn engagement, and affiliate channel traffic into registered users and closed deals.
  - **Leverage AI Capabilities as a Selling Point:** Use successful case studies (like the Amplio list analysis using multiple AI models) to demonstrate IronHub's advanced capabilities to prospective clients.
  - **Community Building:** Continue to grow the audience through targeted marketing and by encouraging the internal team to act as audience amplifiers.

## Strategic Alignment
This project is central to the "Converting traffic into deals" and "Building the community" focus areas detailed in [[02_Areas/ironhub-company]]. It requires strong strategic leadership to align marketing results with sales efforts, a core responsibility outlined in [[02_Areas/ironhub-ceo]].
