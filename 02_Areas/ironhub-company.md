# IronHub

IronHub is an intelligent procurement platform powered by AI, designed to save energy companies time and capital by helping them best utilize their surplus equipment for capital efficiency and sustainability. It is not just a marketplace.

## Core Mission & Value Proposition
- Help energy companies find, evaluate, and source surplus equipment across the IronHub network and beyond.
- Move beyond archaic filtering to allow for free text searches or analysis of uploaded project documents to recommend suitable inventory.
- Save clients time and capital.

## Key Strategic Focus Areas (as of Q2 2026)
1. **Converting traffic into deals:** Bridging the gap between marketing results (high activity/views) and actual sales efforts.
2. **Expanding AI tool adoption:** Leveraging AI to define operations and stay ahead of the technology curve.
3. **Building the community:** Growing the audience and engagement.
4. **Leveraging organic growth:** Utilizing organic search and affiliate/partnership marketplace channels (e.g., Ventured Oilfield, Ask Inc.).

## Key AI Initiatives
- **Fair Market Value (FMV) Calculator 2.0:** Incorporates AI to determine replacement cost of new equipment, apply depreciation factors, and provide real-time comparables from sales data to adjust FMV.
- **AI-Powered Sourcing Tool:** Standardizes inventory options, allows free text searches, and analyzes uploaded project documents to recommend inventory.
- **AI Bulk Import & Enrichment:** Allows users to drop a file (spreadsheet), and the AI parses and enriches the data, providing a confidence level. Future phases aim to integrate OEM data via APIs and Optical Character Recognition (OCR) for nameplates.
