# President & CEO of IronHub

As President and CEO of IronHub, Taylor Assaly is responsible for the overall strategic direction, growth, and operational success of the company. The core mission is to help energy companies best utilize their surplus equipment for capital efficiency and sustainability.

## Core Responsibilities & Focus Areas

### Strategic Leadership & Vision
- Define and drive the company's transition from a traditional marketplace to an intelligent procurement platform powered by AI.
- Ensure the company stays ahead of the technology curve by heavily adopting AI for internal efficiencies and external value creation.
- Set quarterly and annual strategic goals (e.g., the Q2 2026 focus on converting traffic into deals, expanding AI tool adoption, building community, and leveraging organic growth).

### Sales & Conversion Strategy
- Bridge the critical gap between marketing results (high activity and views) and actual sales conversions.
- Develop and implement sales strategies to capitalize on the growing audience and marketplace traffic.
- Lead the effort to turn organic search traffic and affiliate/partnership channels into tangible revenue.

### Technology & Product Oversight
- Oversee the development of key technological initiatives, particularly AI-driven tools.
- Guide the development of the Fair Market Value (FMV) Calculator 2.0 and the AI-Powered Sourcing Tool.
- Ensure technology solutions align with client needs, such as standardizing inventory options and enabling advanced search capabilities (e.g., analyzing uploaded project documents).

### Client & Partner Relations
- Maintain high-level oversight of key client accounts (e.g., Suncor, Senovas, ARC, Paramount, CNU Cook, Secure, Harvest, Canadian Natural, Vermillion, Amplio).
- Leverage successful case studies (like the Amplio list analysis using multiple AI models) to demonstrate capabilities to other clients.
- Foster relationships with affiliate marketplaces and partners (e.g., Ventured Oilfield, Ask Inc.).

### Team Leadership & Communication
- Lead regular "All Hands" team meetings to align the team on performance, goals, and strategic shifts.
- Foster a culture of innovation, accountability, and continuous improvement.
- Encourage team members to act as audience amplifiers by sharing and engaging with company content on platforms like LinkedIn.

## Key Principles (Dan Martell Framework)
- Avoid being a "yes man"; actively challenge ideas and assumptions.
- Act as a mentor, battle-testing strategies and identifying flaws in thinking.
- Prioritize the AI Stack strategy (four engines) for all technology and system development.
