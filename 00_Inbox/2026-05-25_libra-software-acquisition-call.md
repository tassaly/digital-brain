**Key questions Taylor should be prepared for:**
1. What is IronHub's current ARR and revenue mix (transaction fees vs. user licenses)?
2. What is the churn rate on user licenses?
3. Is Taylor looking for a full exit, partial exit, or strategic partnership?
4. What is the current valuation expectation?
