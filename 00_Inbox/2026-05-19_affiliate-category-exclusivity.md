# Affiliate Category Exclusivity — Crusader JV Framework

## Context
Taylor followed up with "Kam" on May 19 regarding a framework discussed the previous week for assigning **Affiliate Category Exclusivity** to IronHub affiliates like Crusader JV.

The concept: affiliates would be granted exclusive rights to represent IronHub within a specific equipment category or geographic territory, creating a structured affiliate channel rather than a free-for-all commission model.

## Why This Matters
This is a direct output of the [[01_Projects/sales-team-restructure]] project — the move toward leaning on affiliates rather than a traditional in-house sales team. Category exclusivity is a powerful incentive for affiliates to invest in the relationship and bring deals proactively.

## Dan Martell Framework Note
This is worth battle-testing before committing. Key questions:
- Does exclusivity create a moat or does it create dependency on affiliates who may underperform?
- How do you enforce exclusivity without creating legal complexity?
- What's the minimum performance threshold to maintain exclusivity?
- Is Crusader JV the right first affiliate to pilot this with, or should you test with someone lower-stakes?

## Status
Framework discussion in progress with Kam. No formal agreement yet.
