## Why This Matters

The delegate access model is a key IronHub differentiator. Engineering firms, procurement consultants, and EPC contractors are natural users of this feature — they need to know what surplus equipment is available from operators before recommending new purchases. This is a direct expansion of the platform's value beyond just buyers and sellers.

**The model:** Operators grant IronHub permission to show their internal surplus to pre-approved third parties (engineering firms, affiliates). The engineering firm sees the inventory; IronHub facilitates the match.
