# Fuelled — Main Marketplace Competitor

Fuelled is a main competitor on the marketplace side. A pointed observation came in via email on May 11, 2026 from Karishma Kulkarni (KNOWIDEA): **"Fuelled is operating as principal — IronHub isn't."**

## What This Means
Operating as **principal** means Fuelled is buying the equipment themselves and reselling it, taking on inventory risk but also capturing the full margin. IronHub currently operates as an **agent/broker**, connecting buyers and sellers without holding inventory.

## Key Questions to Battle-Test
- Is the principal model actually a competitive advantage for Fuelled, or is it a liability (capital tied up in inventory, depreciation risk)?
- Does IronHub's agent model limit deal size and speed, or is it actually more capital-efficient and scalable?
- Should IronHub consider a hybrid model — acting as principal on select high-value, high-confidence deals?
- What does the $10.2B in idle Canadian book value mean for IronHub's opportunity vs. Fuelled's?
